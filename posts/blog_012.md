---
title: Blog_012 - Some days feel impossible but storms don't last forever
date: 2026-06-26
---

It has not been an easy couple of days, the past few ones. Progress is slow, if not non-existent, and even functioning feels hard. It's not easy when it feels like disaster has struck but only very few things are non-surmountable. Losing all of the progress you've done can be detrimental, it feels like you were climbing a mountain and suddenly you get knocked back down to the bottom. Staring at the mountain, at the top, feels impossible but to be honest that's not the place to look at. We need to look at this step by step. Every step, no matter how small, gets us closer to the goal and there is no further down to go than when you are right now. Keep going.
